### ADS (Air Data System)

**Definition**: The Air Data System (ADS) supplies processed air data to the Automatic Flight Control System (AFCS), Electronic Flight Instrument System (EFIS), and navigation systems. The ADS is made up of four Air Data Smart Probes (ADSP), pilot and copilot side pitot and static probes and pilot and copilot side temperature sensors.

**Source**: [Operators Guide - PL Fusion_chunk_0045, Operators Guide - PL Fusion, p.35]

**Status**: DRAFT - REQUIRES HUMAN REVIEW

Processed air data includes uncorrected pressure altitude, total and static pressure, vertical speed, airspeed, Mach, maximum airspeed/Mach, true airspeed, total air temperature, static air temperature, and International Standard Atmosphere (ISA) delta temperature.
