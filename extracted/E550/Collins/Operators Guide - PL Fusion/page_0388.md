MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Visual Annunciations Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

ADI: TAWS PULL UP Warning

#### .

A red PULL UP annunciation is shown below the FD command bar when the TAWS detects an excessive descent rate or rapidly rising terrain. The PULL UP warning ﬂag is accompanied by an aural alert.

ADI: TAWS WINDSHEAR Warning

#### .

A red WINDSHEAR annunciation is shown centrally above the aircraft symbol when the TAWS predicts windshear conditions. The WINDSHEAR warning ﬂag is accompanied by an aural alert.

ADI: PWS Windshear Ahead Warning

#### .

A red WSHR AHEAD annunciation is shown centrally above the aircraft symbol when the optionally installed WXR PWS detects windshear conditions. The WSHR AHEAD warning ﬂag is accompanied by an aural alert.

ADI: Vertical Speed Flag

#### .

A boxed red VS ﬂag is shown in place of the vertical speed scale when vertical speed data is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.
