Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Visual Annunciations

ADI: Autopilot Disconnect

#### .

When the Autopilot (AP) is manually disconnected by the pilot, the AP annunciation in the AFCS mode ﬁeld changes color to red and ﬂashes until the pilot pushes the AP engage or disengage button. When the AP is automatically disconnected by the AFCS, the AP annunciation changes color to red and ﬂashes until the pilot pushes the AP engage or disengage button.

The AP disengage warning is accompanied by an aural.

ADI: Autothrottle Disconnect

When the Autothrottle (AT) is manually disconnected by the pilot, or automatically disconnected by the AFCS, the AT annunciation in the AFCS mode ﬁeld changes color to red and ﬂashes until the pilot pushes the AT engage or disengage button.

ADI: FMS Flag

#### .

A boxed red FMS ﬂag is shown in the map source data block when data from the selected FMS map source is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.
