Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 DISPLAY OPERATION Pro Line Fusion® Avionics GUI Elements

• The Main Menu lower format screen deﬁnes the availability of CHECKLISTS, VIDEO, DLK GWX, TUNE, ACARS, and SATCOM formats and adds synoptics speciﬁc information.

• A toggle control is two or more selectable items. Each unselected item is white text on a gray background, and the selected item is green text on a black background. Position the cursor over the toggle control and select it to toggle through the selectable items. One item is always active.
