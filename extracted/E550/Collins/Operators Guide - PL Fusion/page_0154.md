MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 AFCS Flight Mode Annunciations Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Lateral Mode Field The lateral mode ﬁeld is located immediately left of the AP/AT engagement ﬁeld. The lateral mode ﬁeld is split into three ﬁelds: Approach Lateral Active/Arm, Lateral Arm, and Lateral Active. Active modes are shown in green when sensor/pilot directed, magenta when FMS directed, and armed modes are shown in white.

Vertical Mode Field The vertical mode ﬁeld is located immediately above the right of the AP/AT engagement ﬁeld. The vertical mode ﬁeld is split into three ﬁelds: Altitude Arm, Approach Vertical Active/Arm, and Vertical Active. Active modes are shown in green when sensor/pilot directed, magenta when FMS directed, and armed modes are shown in white.

Half Bank Mode Arc A green arc is shown above the roll scale to indicate that the Half Bank mode is active. The arc extends out 15 degrees either side of the 0 (zero) degrees triangle on the roll scale. The green Half Bank arc is automatically activated as the aircraft climbs through 25 000 feet and is automatically removed as the aircraft descends through 25 000 feet.

Autothrottle Mode Field

The Autothrottle (AT) mode ﬁeld is located immediately to the left of the lateral mode ﬁeld on the ADI format. Active AT modes are shown in green, and armed modes are shown in white. If the throttles can not maintain a manually-commanded speed the AT mode displays a yellow LIM.
