Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Synoptic – Electrical Page

Figure 5-57 Synoptic – Electrical Page

The ELECTRICAL Synoptic page graphically represents the Electrical system. The ELECTRICAL Synoptic page is accessed through the Synoptic drop down menu opened by pushing the SYS QAK.

SELECTIONS:

Buses Buses are depicted by ovals with white text and a colored outline. The oval will be green when energized or white when not energized.

Batteries are depicted by rectangles with green numbers showing volts and temperatures. The voltage and temperature are replaced with a red X when failed.
