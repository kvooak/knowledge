Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics DU – Chart Format

OTHER OTHER is a pushbutton to access the Other Airport Chart menu. The Other airport is entered by the operator with the MKP in the Data Entry window. If no Other airport has been entered, selection of the OTHER button brings up the Data Entry window.

BROWSE BROWSE is a DSK icon to show that the outer knob of the DSK may be used to browse through the various instrument approach/departure plates for the selected airport.

ZOOM ZOOM is a DSK icon to show that the inner knob of the DSK may be used to zoom in or zoom out the current chart.

RWY RWY is a single stage toggle control for the Target Runway Identiﬁcation function of the optional Surface Management System (SMS). The RWY button is inactive if the runway reference conditions are unavailable.

Pan Control The pan control is a button with a hand symbol on it. The pan control is a single-state toggle (toggle on/toggle off), and times out (toggle off) after 2 seconds. When the chart is zoomed completely out (or the Fit control on the Chart Task menu was selected) and the pan control is toggled on, moving the cursor instead pans the current chart.
