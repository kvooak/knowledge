MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Checklist Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• Auto Synoptic — In some cases, a checklist may contain a hyper link to one of the synoptic formats. If the Auto Synoptic checkbox is selected and a pilot comes to a checklist item with one of these links, the avionics will automatically display the linked synoptic on the quarter format below the checklist. For the synoptic to automatically be displayed, the following conditions must be satisﬁed:

1. The Auto Synoptic checkbox under the FCN drop down menu has been selected.

2. A synoptic link has been assigned to a checklist item.

3. The pilot advances to a linked checklist item.

4. Only one checklist window is displayed in the cockpit in an upper quarter format.
