MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 ADI – Altitude Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

RA/BARO MIN Alert The RA/BAROMIN alert is shown to alert the pilots that the aircraft has reached the selected RA or BARO MIN altitude setting. The RA/BARO MIN alert is inhibited on the ground, and is inhibited after ground to air transition until the aircraft has climbed 50 feet above the selected RA/BARO MIN.

• When the aircraft descends to the RA MIN altitude, a boxed, yellow MIN ﬂag displays to the left of the altitude scale. The MIN ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then remains steady.

• When the aircraft descends to the BARO MIN altitude, a boxed, yellow MIN ﬂag displays to the left of the altitude scale. The MIN ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then remains steady.
