Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 AFCS OPERATION Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Approach Modes

Approach Checklist Messages

Message Priority Meaning

APPR 2 NOT CAPABLE 0 Approach 2 is not capable.

CHECK NAV SRC 1 The PFD indicated by the source status arrow is selected LOC and the other PFD has the same LOC source or a different NAV source.

CHECK LOC TUNE 2 The ILS frequencies are not identical.

CHECK COURSE 3 The PFDs are not displaying the same selected course.

CHECK ADS REV 4 The PFDs are not displaying independent ADS data.

CHECK AHRS REV 5 The PFDs are not displaying independent AHRS data.

CHECK FLAP 6 Flap lever position is not equal to 3 and radio altitude is lower than or equal to 1500 ft.

#### APPROACH 1 OPERATION

Approach 1 operation is similar to approach 2, with the exception that either the system is not APPR 2 capable or one of the necessary selections for Approach 2 has not been made. This occurs when:

• APPR 2 is NOT capable

• LOC is NOT active or is the view source on both PFDs

• Same LOC selected in both PFDs

• LOC frequencies are NOT equal

• Same AHRS or ADS sources are selected on the PFDs
