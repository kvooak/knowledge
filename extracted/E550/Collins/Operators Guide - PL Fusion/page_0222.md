MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Synoptic – Status Page Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Figure 5-70 Synoptic – Status Page

The STATUS Synoptic page graphically represents additional aircraft systems. The STATUS Synoptic page is accessed through the Synoptic drop down menu accessed through SYS QAK. The STATUS Synoptic page depicts the cockpit doors and temperatures in a cabin outline and readouts and gauges for additional data.

SELECTIONS:

Doors The aircraft doors are depicted on the cabin outline as colored squares.

Flight Status The ﬂight status box gives ﬂight identiﬁcation, outside temperatures with digital readouts for Total Air Temperature (TAT), Static Air Temperature (SAT), and International Standard Atmosphere (ISA), as well as gross weight.
