## CONTROLS AND INDICATORS

### AUDIO CONTROL PANEL

Two Audio Control Panel (ACP) units are installed in the main instrument panel outboard of the displays. The ACP provides the selection and volume control for installed voice and other audio communication equipment. The ACP performs all necessary signal processing for communication between the installed radio systems and peripheral devices (for example, speakers, headsets).
