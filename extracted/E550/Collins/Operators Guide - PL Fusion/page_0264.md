MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Checklist – Components Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Figure 5-96 Checklist – Conditional Button

Selecting an unchecked item in the checklist displays a green check mark in the selected box and change the font color to green. The focus indicator and current line indicator moves to the next unchecked item.

Conditional items allowing two choices are incorporated in the checklist. Each selection in a conditional item may choose a different checklist path.

Sometimes a timer can be associated with a checklist item. If a timer exists with a checklist item, the timer will be displayed in the top right corner of the checklist window.
