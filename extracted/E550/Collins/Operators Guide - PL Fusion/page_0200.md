MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 EICAS Format Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• Spoilers – The status of the spoilers is depicted with a small rectangle on top of the wing shape, as well as a white SPLR legend.

• SPDBRK – When the speed brake handle is engaged a white SPDBRK annunciation shows inside a gray box.

• Flaps – The status of the ﬂaps is depicted with a small pointer at the trailing edge of the wing shape, a readout, a white scale, and a gray white legend. Flap position is also given inside a gray box behind a white FLAP legend.

• Horizontal stabilizer trim – Horizontal stabilizer trim is depicted with a vertical analog scale, a green takeoff band, and a digital readout inside a pointer. A gray PITCH legend is shown above the scale, a white Nose Up (UP) legend is shown at the top, and a white Nose Down (DN) legend is shown at the bottom of the scale.

• Rudder trim – Rudder trim is depicted with a horizontal analog scale, a pointer, and a gray YAW legend.

CAS Message List The CAS message list shows in the upper right corner of the EICAS display. Four categories of CAS messages show, in the following priority order:

• Warning – Warning messages are the highest priority, and display in red.

• Caution – Caution messages show in yellow.

• Advisory – Advisory messages display in cyan.
