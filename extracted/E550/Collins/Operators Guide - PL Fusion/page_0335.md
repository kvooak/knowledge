Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Chart – NOTAMs Window

### CHART – NOTAMS WINDOW

Figure 5-142 Chart – NOTAMs Window

The Chart NOTAM window displays NOTAMs for the selected airport. The Chart NOTAM window is accessed through the Chart NOTAMs submenu link on the Airport Chart menus.

The chart NOTAMs are listed in the following order:

1. By Type: Terminal NOTAMs ﬁrst, then General, then

2. By Effectivity: Permanent NOTAMs ﬁrst, then temporary, then

3. By Begin Date: immediately ﬁrst, then by date (earliest to latest).
