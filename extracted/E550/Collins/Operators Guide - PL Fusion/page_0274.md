MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Map – Menu Bar Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

The PREV WPT button centers the map at the previous waypoint, and the previous waypoint displays in the readout to the right of the button. The NEXT WPT button centers the map at the next waypoint, and the next waypoint displays in the readout to the right of the button. The TO WPT button centers the map at the current waypoint. The CTR WPT button displays when a waypoint is entered in the text entry box to the right. The CTR WPT button centers the map at the entered waypoint. The CTR A/C centers the map at the current position of the aircraft.

The Map Center dialog box closes when one of the buttons is selected, or when the gray X is selected or the ESC button on the CCP is pushed.

Map Symbol The Map Symbol menu shows when the SYMBOL button is selected from the Map Menu Bar. The Map Symbol menu consists of a list of check boxes.
