MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Video – Control Panel Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

The Video Control panel is a dialog box to adjust the display settings of the Video format.

SELECTIONS:

Brightness The brightness selection consists of + (plus) and −(minus) buttons to adjust the brightness of the video display.

Contrast The contrast selection consists of + (plus) and −(minus) buttons to adjust the contrast of the video display.
