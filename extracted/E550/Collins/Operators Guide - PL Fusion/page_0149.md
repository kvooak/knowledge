Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics ADI – Lateral/Vertical Deviation

Marker Beacons The marker beacon indicator is shown on the right side of the attitude indicator. The outer marker is a cyan, boxed OM annunciation that ﬂashes on and off when the aircraft is passing over an outer marker beacon. The middle marker is a yellow, boxed MM annunciation that ﬂashes on and off when the aircraft is passing over a middle marker beacon. The inner marker is a white, boxed IM annunciation that ﬂashes on and off when the aircraft is passing over an inner marker beacon. If more than one marker beacon is active at the same time, the indicator alternates between the active markers.

RNP Readout The Required Navigation Perfor- mance (RNP) is shown in magenta to the right of the lateral deviation scale if it is in the navigation database. ANG is shown for LPV approaches.

Ground Speed GS is shown to the left of the lateral deviation scale in green. The speed is preceeded by a white GS.
