### CURSOR CONTROL PANEL

A Cursor Control Panel (CCP) is installed on each side of the center console. The CCP includes a trackball for controlling a cursor on the primary displays, provides a means of selecting items in conjunction with menus and interactive display items, supplies convenient PTT switches, and provides another means of data entry through the double stack knob.
