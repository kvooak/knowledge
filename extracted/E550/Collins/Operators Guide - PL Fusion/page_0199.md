Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics EICAS Format

• Cabin altitude is given in green numbers behind a white ALT legend.

• Cabin rate of change displays in green numbers behind a white RATE legend.

• Cabin differential pressure is shown in green behind a white ΔP legend, and given in PSI.

• Landing Field Elevation (LFE) is shown in green behind a white LFE legend. LFE is changed using the PFD VSPEEDS/LFE dialog box.

Aircraft Control Indications

The aircraft control indications display consists of gauges for landing gear, speed brakes/spoilers, ﬂaps, pitch trim, and rudder trim.

• Landing Gear – Landing gear is depicted using green circles with green DN when landing gear is down. White UP shows in the center of a white square when landing gear are up. Gear in transit shows as a circle with cross hatching. If the gear don’t match the position of the handle, the white LG legend turns yellow.
