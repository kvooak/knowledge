Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Video – Control Panel

The Video Source menu is a drop down list to select the video source to be shown on the Video format. The Video Source menu is a multi-state toggle, only one video source may be shown at a time. The selections on the Video Source menu reﬂect the current equipment installed on the aircraft. Only the currently installed cameras are listed. The non-EVS cameras may be named by the customer or OEM, and these names show on the Video Source menu. If a camera image is failed or unavailable, a white Video Image Not Available message shows in the Video format.

SELECTIONS:

Camera The Camera selections conﬁgure the Video format to show video signal from the selected camera. The Camera options are listed as installed, and can be named by the customer or OEM.

EVS The EVS selection conﬁgures the Video format to show video signal from the EVS. The EVS selection is unavailable if the optional EVS is not installed.
