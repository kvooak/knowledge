MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Map – Radar Overlay Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

N

NOTE

Extending outward from the aircraft symbol is a blanked range area. The size of this blanked range area equals 0.6 NM. The blanked range does not display any WX returns.

The weather radar overlay is selected on the DU Map Overlay menu.

SELECTIONS:

WXR Colors Detectable weather is shown in up to four colors: green, yellow, red, and magenta. The lowest precipitation rates are shown in green. The highest precipitation rates and detected turbulence are shown in magenta. Refer to the table that follows for details on the relationship between precipitation rates and display colors.

WXR Colors

Display Color Precipitation Rate

No display Less than 0.03 in/hr

Green 0.03 to 0.15 in/hr

Yellow 0.15 to 0.50 in/hr

Red 0.50 to 2.0 in/hr

Magenta 2.0 in/hr and greater

NOTE: Turbulence is also shown in magenta. Turbulence is only shown out to 40 NM, regardless of current selected range.
