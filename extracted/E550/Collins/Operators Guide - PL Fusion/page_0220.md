MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Synoptic – Hydraulics Page Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Figure 5-68 Synoptic – Hydraulics Page

The HYDRAULICS Synoptic page graphically represents the hydraulic system. The HYDRAULICS Synoptic page is accessed through the Synoptic drop down menu accessed with the SYS QAK. The HYDRAULICS synoptic page depicts the three hydraulic systems with a list of the powered components below each system.

SELECTIONS:

Temperatures The hydraulic ﬂuid temperatures are depicted by digital readouts.

Quantities The hydraulic ﬂuid quantities are depicted by digital readouts and graphical level indicators and are measured in percent of full.

Pressures The hydraulic ﬂuid pressures are depicted by digital readouts.
