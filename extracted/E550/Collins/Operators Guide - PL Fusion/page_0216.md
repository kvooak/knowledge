MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Synoptic – Flight Control Page Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Spoilers The spoilers are depicted by rectangular icons above the wings with position pointers. Green rectangles indicate spoilers are working correctly, yellow rectangles with a X indicate they are not working correctly, and no rectangle indicates the Synoptic page is not receiving inputs. Spoilers are also indicated on either side of a SPOILER legend.

Flaps The ﬂaps are depicted on the ground by a white FLAPS legend between two squares. The actual position of the ﬂaps show beside the squares as well.

Elevators The elevators are depicted by icons on the tail surfaces. Green rectangles indicate the elevator is working correctly, yellow rectangles with a X indicate they are not working correctly, and no rectangle indicates the Synoptic page is not receiving inputs. The elevator is also indicated on either side of an ELEVATOR legend.

Rudder The rudder is depicted by a rectangular icon on the tail. Green rectangles indicate the rudder is working correctly, yellow rectangles with a X indicate they are not working correctly, and no rectangle indicates the Synoptic page is not receiving inputs. The rudder is also indicated on either side of an RUDDER legend.
