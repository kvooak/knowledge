Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics ADI – Vspeed References

VR VR – Rotation speed. This reference is shown on the ADI airspeed scales as a horizontal line with a R legend.

V2 V2 – Takeoff safety speed. This reference is shown on the ADI airspeed scales as a horizontal line with a 2 legend.

VFS VFS – Final takeoff speed. This reference is shown on the ADI airspeed scales as a horizontal line with a FS legend, during both takeoff and approach.

VAC VAC – Approach climb speed. This reference is shown on the ADI airspeed scales as a horizontal line with an AC legend.

VAP VAP – Approach speed. This reference is shown on the ADI airspeed scales as a horizontal line with an AP legend.

VREF VREF – Approach Airspeed in the landing conﬁguration. This reference is shown on the ADI airspeed scales as a horizontal line with a REF legend, during approach only.

Table The Vspeed table can display at the bottom of the airspeed scale. As airspeed increases above 40 knots, the speed reference table scrolls smoothly off of the bottom of the airspeed scale in conjunction with the increasing airspeed. The Vspeed legends and their associated values are shown.
