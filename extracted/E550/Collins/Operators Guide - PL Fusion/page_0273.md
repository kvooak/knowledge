SELECTIONS:

Flight Plan Toggle The ﬂight plan toggle selects between the active and secondary ﬂight plans. When more than one ﬂight plan toggle shows on a DU, the selections are synced.

The active selection is depicted in green text on a black background, and the inactive selection is depicted in white text on a gray background.

Map Center The Map Center dialog box shows when the map Center (CTR) button on the Menu Bar is selected. The Map Center button is only shown in Plan Map mode.
