Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Synoptic – Fuel Page

Horizontal Stabilizer The horizontal stabilizer is depicted on the ground by a white HSTAB legend between two squares. The actual position of the stabilizer shows beside the squares as well.

Surface Awareness The aircraft limits elevator, roll, and rudder control surface authority as it gets faster in order not to over control. The amounts of control are indicated in an oval labeled SURFACE AWARENESS. If the control limiters are in effect the numbers show as black inside a yellow box.
