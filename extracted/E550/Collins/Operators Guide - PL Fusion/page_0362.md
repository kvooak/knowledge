MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 DU – Universal Graphical Weather Format Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Figure 5-169 XM GWX – Legend Submenu (5/5)

The Legend submenu depicts the symbology used to depict METAR, SIGMET, SCIT Tops, NEXRAD, Winds Aloft, Icing, Satellite, and Turbulence overlays and images. A paging control is used to view all of the GWX legend symbology.

### DU – UNIVERSAL GRAPHICAL WEATHER FORMAT The Universal Graphical Weather (GWX) MFW format displays uplinked weather information. Universal GWX is referred to as Datalink (DLK) in this guide and the on-screen menus. The information used for the XM GWX format is received through a satellite data link receiver.

The DLK GWX format displays a banner across the bottom of the weather image. The banner displays the region, product short name, valid date and time of the image, and age of the image.

The DLK GWX can show the following weather images:

• NEXRAD

• Tops and movement

• Weather depiction (ceiling/visibility summary)
