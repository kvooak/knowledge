MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 ADI – Attitude Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• The following information is kept when an ADI is decluttered:

• Airspeed (IAS tape, Mach digital readout, airspeed trend vector, overspeed Indication, low speed indication, and IAS fail ﬂag)

• Attitude (aircraft symbol, sky/ground indication, horizon line, pitch tape, roll scale, roll pointer, and slip/skid indicator)

• Altitude (altitude tape, altitude trend vector, altitude fail ﬂag, barometric pressure readout, and barometric pressure legend)

• Vertical speed (vertical speed and vertical speed fail ﬂag)

• Direction (compass, current heading readout, and heading fail ﬂag)

• Unusual attitude component

• Flight Mode Annunciation (FMA) ﬁeld (AP engage/disengage, AT active/disengage, and couple arrow)

• TAWS ﬁeld (terrain alert map, HSI range rings, and range readout).
