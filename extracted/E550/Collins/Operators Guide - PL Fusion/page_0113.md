Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics ADI – Attitude

Figure 5-10 HUD – Unusual Attitude (Roll)

An aircraft symbol is shown in the center of the attitude indicator. The attitude indicator display includes a roll scale, a pitch scale, and slip/skid indication. Chevrons show for excessive pitch angles. The display is decluttered if the aircraft is ﬂown in an unusual attitude.

Automatic Flight Control System (AFCS) Flight Director (FD) cues are also shown on the attitude display. The FD cue shows the needed pitch and roll guidance information from the AFCS. TCAS Resolution Advisory (RA) pitch cues are shown on the attitude display and ADI.

Attitude indications on the optional Head-Up Display (HUD) are functionally identical to the attitude indications on the ADI.
