Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Video Format

### VIDEO FORMAT

The Video format displays video feed from up to 3 cameras, or 2 cameras and Enhanced Vision System (EVS) per DU (12 video sources for L450/500 since 4 DUs). The EVS uses a Forward Looking Infrared (FLIR) camera to generate a video display in the cockpit. The EVS system can increase pilot situational awareness of the terminal environment during low light, night, or low visibility conditions.

If no video cameras or EVS are installed, the Video format is unavailable.

SELECTIONS:

Window Format Button

The window format button is a combo box to conﬁgure the DU formats.

Video Source Video source is a list access button to open the Video Source drop down menu.

Mode and failure annunciations may be shown on the Video format, as applicable.

Video Format Annunciations

Text Color Location Description

EVS Green Upper left Indicates the EVS is the active video source.

EVS CAL Green Upper left Shown when the EVS is performing a calibration.
