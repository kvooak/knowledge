Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics ADI – Lateral/Vertical Deviation

Figure 5-18 HUD – Lateral/Vertical Deviation Indicator

The ADI displays the active Navigation (NAV) information as lateral and vertical deviation scales positioned below and to the right of the attitude display. The marker beacon indicator shows part-time to the left of the vertical deviation scale.

Deviation indications on the optional Head-Up Display (HUD) are functionally identical to the deviation indications on the ADI.
