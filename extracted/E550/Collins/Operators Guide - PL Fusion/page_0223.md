Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Synoptic – Status Page

Electric The electric summary is located in the box labeled ELEC. The electric summary shows the voltage of both aircraft batteries.

ENG OIL The page gives engine oil quantity as triangles on a sliding scale with numerical readouts on either side of a gray QT legend.

Oxygen The oxygen summary is located in the box labeled OXY. The level is indicated by a triangle on the scale, as well as a digital readout with a gray PSI legend.

APU The APU summary is located in the box labeled APU. The Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) is a digital readout, Revolutions Per Minute (RPM) is a digital readout of the percentage, and oil pressure is depicted as either OK or LO QTY.

BRAKES The BRAKES section shows both left (SYS1) and right (SYS2) brake systems. Each system is further broken down to accumulator pressures on the top scales, as well as Outboard (OB) and Inboard (IB) brake temperatures on the bottom scales.
