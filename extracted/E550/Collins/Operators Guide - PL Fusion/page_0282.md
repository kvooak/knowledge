MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Map – Terrain Overlay Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Alerted Terrain Colors and Type

Color Type

Red – High Intensity Terrain Threat Area – Warning

Yellow – High Intensity Terrain Threat Area – Caution

Peak Indicators The highest elevation points of the lateral map terrain overlay are identiﬁed with two peak indicator symbols. The inner peak indicator displays the highest elevation with the half range ring. The outer peak indicator displays the highest elevation from the half range ring to the edge of the display. If two or more points are the same elevation, the peak indicator displays for the point closest to the aircraft.

When the peak indicator location is off the viewable area of the map, the peak indicator is parked on the edge of the viewable area, on the correct azimuth, shown in magenta.
