CONTROLS AND INDICATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Display Control Panel Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

V/L VOR/Localizer But- ton

Push the V/L button to change the primary NAV source to VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) or Localizer (LOC). VOR or LOC is selected based on the frequency placed in the NAV position. Push the V/L button to select the on-side VOR/LOC. Further pushes toggle between the cross-side VOR/LOC and the on-side VOR/LOC.

FMS FMS Button

Push the FMS button to select the Flight Management System (FMS) as the primary NAV source. The left DCP selects FMS1, and the right DCP selects FMS2 (if installed). Further pushes toggle between the cross-side FMS and the on-side FMS.

HSI HSI Format Selec- tion Button

The HSI button selects between the HSI formats. HSI formats include Clean (no background), Compass (range ring with TCAS and WX overlays possible), and Map (FMS ﬂight information shown).

RANGE / TILT WX Radar Range / Tilt Knob

The Range/Tilt button allows the crew to adjust the weather radar for aircraft attitude and to focus on possible inclement weather. The outer control (larger knob) adjusts the range. The inner control (smaller knob) adjusts the tilt.

STBY WXR ON Weather Standby/On Power Button

Push the STBY WXR ON button to change the WXR status from standby to on, and back.
