MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 EICAS Format Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• N1 digital readout – The N1 digital readout displays the current N1 in green in the center of the N1 scale.

• Command arc – A cyan arc is shown on the N1 scale at the current N1 command throttle position.

• Target N1 mode – The inverse yellow or cyan target N1 mode is shown above the white N1 legend.

• Target N1 bug – A V shaped bug is shown on the N1 scale at the target N1 rating. The target N1 bug shows in cyan when either an automatic or a manual thrust mode is selected.

• Target N1 digital readout – The target N1 digital readout displays the target N1 rating above each N1 gauge. The target N1 digital readout shows in cyan when either an automatic or a manual thrust mode is selected.

• Engine status – The engine status may be shown on top of the analog N1 gauges. Possible status indications include a cyan OFF, green RSV, or inverse yellow FAIL.

• N1 Redline – The N1 scale includes a red line to mark the maximum N1 of the engine.

• Max Thrust Reference – The max thrust reference is the green tick mark on the arc.

ITT The ITT display consists of analog scales and digital readouts for each engine, on either side of a white ITT legend.
