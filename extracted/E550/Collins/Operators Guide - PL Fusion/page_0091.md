### HUD BRIGHTNESS CONTROLS (OPTIONAL)

The HUD brightness control panel for the optional HGS is located on the PDU. Controls are provided for the video image and the HUD symbology.

CALLOUTS:

IMAGE Image Knob

The IMAGE knob controls the brightness of the EVS image on the HUD display.

HUD HUD Knob

The HUD knob controls the brightness of both the symbology and video image on the HUD display.
