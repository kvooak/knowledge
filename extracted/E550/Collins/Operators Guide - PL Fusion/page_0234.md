MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Tuning – COM Control Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Figure 5-78 Tuning – COM3 Control

The COM Control submenu provides advanced controls to modify VHF settings. The COM Control menu is accessed by selecting a gray COM button on the Tuning format. COM1, COM2, and COM3 Control submenus are available.

SELECTIONS:

Tune Tune the standby frequency by positioning the cursor over the standby frequency digits and using the DSK.

↕(Transfer) ↕is a selection to transfer the active and standby frequencies.

FREQ SEL FREQ SEL is a multi-state toggle to select the frequency spacing between 8.33 kHz and 25 kHz spacing.

Squelch Squelch is a multi-state toggle to enable or disable squelch on the VHF radio.
