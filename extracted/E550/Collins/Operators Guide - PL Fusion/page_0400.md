MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Visual Annunciations Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

ADI: TAWS WINDSHEAR Alert

#### .

A yellow WINDSHEAR annunciation is shown centrally above the aircraft symbol when the TAWS predicts windshear conditions may exist. The WINDSHEAR caution ﬂag is accompanied by an aural alert.

ADI: PWS Windshear Ahead Alert

#### .

A yellow WSHR AHEAD annunciation is shown centrally above the aircraft symbol when the optionally installed WXR predicts windshear conditions may exist. The WSHR AHEAD caution ﬂag is accompanied by an aural alert.

ADI: Vertical Deviation Miscompare Flag

#### .

The vertical deviation miscompare ﬂag shows when the pilot and copilot vertical deviation bars do not show the same deviation. The miscompare alert ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then shows steady.

ADI: Vertical Navigation Comparator Flag

#### .

The VNV comparator ﬂag is a caution to the ﬂight crew that the VNAV reference values being shown on the ADI may be in error on one side or the other.
