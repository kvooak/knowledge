CONTROLS AND INDICATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Flight Control Panel Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

SRC Source Switch

The SRC button determines from which PDF the AFGS will extracts its inputs and references. When in dual lane AFGS architecture conﬁguration, the SRC pushbutton deﬁnes from which PFD the ﬂight guidance application will receive inputs for the active AP commands computation, since the ﬁght guidance commands on each PFD will be calculated by independent ﬂight guidance applications, which, on their turns, consume their inputs from its on-side PFD.

ALT Altitude Hold Mode Select Button

The ALT selects and deselects the Altitude Hold mode. When in Altitude Hold mode, the AFCS generates commands to maintain a target barometric altitude. The inner button (PUSH CANCEL) toggles the altitude unit between feet and meters.

ALT Altitude Setting Control Knob and Readout

The inner knob selects the altitude increasing/decreasing 100 feet or 10 meters according to unit selected through the inner button.

The outer knob selects the altitude increasing/decreasing 1000 feet or 100 meters according to unit selected through the inner button.

FLCH Flight Level Change Mode Select Button

The FLCH button selects and deselects the Flight Level Change mode. When in FLCH mode, the AFCS generates commands to maintain a selected target speed while climbing or descending towards the selected altitude reference.
