### TUNING – NAV CONTROL

Figure 5-80 Tuning – NAV Control

The NAV Control submenu provides advanced controls to modify NAV settings. The NAV Control menu is accessed by selecting a gray NAV button on the Tuning format. NAV1 and NAV2 Control submenus are available.

SELECTIONS:

Tune Tune the standby frequency by positioning the cursor over the standby frequency digits and using the DSK.

↕(Transfer) ↕is a selection to transfer the active and standby frequencies.
