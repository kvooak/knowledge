CONTROLS AND INDICATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Multifunction Keyboard Panel Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

When there is no data in the scratchpad, pushing the CLR/DEL button enters the word DELETE into the scratchpad. This can then be transferred to various functions or data ﬁelds on the FMS window to delete the data currently entered or shown for that function or ﬁeld.

ENTER Enter Button

The ENTER button enters the scratchpad text into the selected display entry ﬁeld.

CHART Chart Quick Access Key

The CHART QAK displays the chart format on the on-side MFW of the bottom display unit (DU4) during normal operation. The on-side cursor is positioned on the chart format.

SYS System Quick Ac- cess Key

Pushing the SYS QAK displays the STATUS synoptic on the on-side lower quarter format of the MFW on the bottom display unit (DU4). Subsequent pushes of the SYS QAK cycles through the synoptic pages.
