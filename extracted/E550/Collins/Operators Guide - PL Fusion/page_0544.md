APPENDIX C Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 CPDLC Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

NAVAID:

• NAVAID identiﬁer expressed in alphanumeric characters

• Displays from 1 – 4 characters.

BEARING (Position):

• Magnetic bearing in degrees

• Displays from 001 – 360 in one degree increments.

FAC DESIGN (Unit Name):

• Ground facility designation

• Displays from 4 – 8 alphanumeric characters.

FACILITY:

• Ground facility function

• Includes CENTER, CLEARANCE DELIVERY, APPROACH, DEPARTURE, TOWER, CONTROL, FINAL, RADIO, or GROUND CONTROL.

FAC ID (Unit Name):

• Ground facility identiﬁer (name)

• 3 – 18 characters.

VHF (Frequency):

• Radio frequency

• Displays from 118.00 – 136.990.

• Resolution of .005.

IAS (Speed):

• Indicated airspeed

• Displays from 0 – 400

• Resolution of 1.

MACH (Speed):

• Mach indicated

• Displays from 0.5 – 4

• Resolution of .001.
