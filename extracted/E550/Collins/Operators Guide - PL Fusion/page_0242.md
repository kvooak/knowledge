### TUNING – XPDR/TCAS CONTROL

Figure 5-83 Tuning – XPDR/TCAS Control

The XPDR/TCAS Control submenu provides advanced controls to modify XPDR/TCAS settings. The XPDR/TCAS Control menu is accessed by selecting the gray XPDR TCAS button on the Tuning format.
