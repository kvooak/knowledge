MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 PFD – HUD Format Dialog Box Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

SELECTIONS:

COMBINER MODE

COMBINER MODE allows the pilot to select the display mode for the HUD symbology. The FULL selection shows the standard HUD layout. The DECLUTTER selection shows the decluttered layout, which minimizes the non-essential information for critical phases of ﬂight. Refer to the ﬁgure on page 5-77 for the decluttered layout. When in AUTO selection, the HUD display will automatically switch to declutter display when the following conditions are met: Flap Handle is in D or E position, landing gear is down and radio Altitude is less than 1500 ft.

The HUD will stay in declutter mode until go around is initiated or aircraft is on ground and ground speed is less than 60 knots.

FPV CAGE FPV CAGE is a multi-state toggle to cage and uncage the FPV on the HUD.

IMAGE The IMAGE selection allows the pilot to select the video overlay on the HUD from Synthetic Vision System (SVS), Enhanced Vision System (EVS), or OFF.
