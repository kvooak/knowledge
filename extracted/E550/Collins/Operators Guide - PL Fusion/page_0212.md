MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Synoptic – Environmental Control System Page Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Duct Duct temperature is depicted in green numbers for both the CKPT and Cabin in both °C and °F under a white DUCT legend.

Recirculating Fan The ECS Synoptic displays if the recirculating fan, labeled RECIRC in white, is operating. If the fan is operating it is shown in green. If not in operation it is shown in white.

Air Conditioning Pack

The air conditioning pack is depicted as a square with the legend PACK. The legend and outline show in green when operating, white when not operating.

Air Conditioning Pack Backup

The air conditioning pack backup is depicted as a square with the legend PACK BACKUP. The legend and outline show in green when operating and white when not operating.

Flow Control Valve Two Flow Controls Valve (FCV) depictions let the crew know if bleed air is entering the air conditioning packs.

Pressure Relief and Shut Off Valve

The bleed air pressure can be shut off using the Pressure Relief and Shut Off Valve (PRSOV) or the Auxiliary Power Unit (APU) valve (labeled APU VLV) from each source to the rest of the system.

Ground Cart The ECS Synoptic displays the external ground cart as a square with wheels labeled GND CART. The legend and outline show in green when in operation and white when not in operation.
