Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics HSI Format

Vector Wind The vector wind direction arrow rotates 360 degrees to represent the wind direction relative to the aircraft (arrow pointing to 6 o’clock equals direct head wind) and the readout indicates the speed.

XY Wind The wind is broken down into two components: cross wind and head wind/tail wind components. Cross wind is indicated by the upper arrow and readout: arrow pointing right equals wind from the right, arrow pointing left equals wind from the left. Head wind/tail wind is indicated by the lower arrow and readout (6 o’clock equals head wind, 12 o’clock tail wind).

Weather Radar and Lightning Overlays

Weather Radar and Lightning overlays can be shown on the HSI display. HSI overlays are selected with the PFD/HSI Format Task menu.

Terrain Overlay Terrain overlay can be selected for display by selecting TERRAIN from the HSI format dialog box. HSI Terrain overlay is default to on at power up.

TCAS Trafﬁc Over- lay

The TCAS trafﬁc overlay can be selected using the TCAS button on the DCP or from the HSI format dialog box.
