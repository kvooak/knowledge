Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 OVERVIEW – SYSTEM COMPONENTS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics System Description

Pilot Display Unit (PDU) (Optional)

As part of the optional HGS, the Pilot Display Unit (PDU) contains the electronic circuitry to display the ﬂight symbology and video image on Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) and projects the ﬂight symbology and video image on the combiner glass. The PDU also contains electronic circuitry that controls the intensity of the projected symbology and video image. The PDU is installed in such away that the ﬂight symbology and the video image are conformed to real world when looking though the combiner likes the artiﬁcial horizon. The brightness control for the HUD display and video image are located on the PDU.

Power Supply Unit (PSU) (Optional)

Part of the optional HGS, the Power Supply Unit (PSU) converts and conditions aircraft power for the PDU.

Radio Altimeter (RAD ALT) The Radio Altimeter (RAD ALT) measures height Above Ground Level (AGL) from 0 to 2500 feet (0 to 762 meters). The RAD ALT uses this input to generate digital altitude data that is shown on the AFD, and for use by the hazard avoidance subsystems. Two RAD ALT transceivers are installed.
