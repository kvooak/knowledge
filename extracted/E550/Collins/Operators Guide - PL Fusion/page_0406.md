MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Visual Annunciations Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

#### HUD – ANNUNCIATIONS

HUD: EVS Failure Annunciation

#### EVS (boxed, ﬂashing)

The boxed, ﬂashing EVS fail annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when a failure is detected in the EVS video stream.

HUD: EVS Annunciation

#### EVS The EVS annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when EVS is the active video source.

HUD: EVS Alignment Mode Annunciation

#### EVS ALIGN MODE The EVS ALIGN MODE annunciation is shown in the center of the HUD when the EVS has been set to alignment mode from the OMS page.

HUD: EVS Calibration Annunciation

#### EVS CAL The EVS CAL annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when the EVS is performing a calibration.

HUD: EVS Clear Annunciation

#### EVS CLR The EVS CLR annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when the EVS video has been cleared by the pilot.
