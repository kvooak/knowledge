Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Document Reader – Document Menu

The Document Reader format provides a means to show documents. The Document Reader format may also be commanded to open the Fault Isolation Manual (FIM) to an error code speciﬁed by the OMS.

### DOCUMENT READER – DOCUMENT MENU

Figure 5-181 Document Reader – Document Menu

The Document menu provides for selection from available documents.

While airborne, the Document menu lists the available ﬂight manuals and pilot guides. When on the ground, the Document menu lists the maintenance manuals in addition to the ﬂight manuals and pilot guides.
