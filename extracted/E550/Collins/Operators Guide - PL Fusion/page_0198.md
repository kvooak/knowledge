MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 EICAS Format Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• The oil temperature display consists of green digital readouts for each engine and a white OIL TEMP legend.

• The oil pressure display consists of digital readouts for each engine and a white OIL PRES legend. Oil pressure is given in Pounds per Square Inch (PSI).

• Engine Vibration (VIB) is given in green digital readouts for each engine and a white VIB legend.

Fuel Display The fuel display shows the Fuel Flow (FF) and Fuel Quantity (FQ) remaining.

• The fuel ﬂow display consists of digital readouts for each engine and a white FF and gray PPH or KPH legend.

• The fuel quantity display consists of digital readouts for the right and left wing fuel tanks and a readout for the total fuel quantity. Total fuel quantity shows inside a white box Tank quantities display on either side of a white FQ and gray LBS or KG legend.

Cabin Altitude The EICAS also tracks the cabin altitude, rate of change, differential pressure, and landing ﬁeld elevation.
