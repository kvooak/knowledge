Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Visual Annunciations

HUD: EVS Initialize Annunciation

#### EVS INIT The EVS INIT annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD during EVS initialization.

HUD: EVS Temperature Annunciation

#### EVS TEMP The EVS TEMP annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when the EVS video has been removed due to a thermal shutdown.

HUD: HUD Alignment Mode Annunciation

#### HUD ALIGN MODE The HUD ALIGN MODE annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when the HUD is set to electronic boresight alignment mode from the OMS page.

HUD: SVS Failure Annunciation

#### SVS (boxed, ﬂashing)

The boxed, ﬂashing SVS fail annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when a failure is detected in the SVS video stream.

HUD: SVS Annunciation

#### SVS The SVS annunciation is shown in the upper left of the HUD when SVS is the active video source.
