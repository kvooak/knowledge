MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 ADI – Attitude Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

SELECTIONS:

Pitch Tape The pitch tape moves vertically with respect to the aircraft symbol, and rotates in roll. The pitch tape displays +5.5 and -8 degrees of visible pitch when in ADI Normal Area and CVS mode is SVS. The pitch tape displays approximately +17 and -23 degrees of visible pitch when CVS mode is off.

Roll Scale The roll scale is located at the top of the attitude display and makes an arc between the airspeed and altitude displays. The roll scale displays 360 degrees of roll, with 10, 20, and 30 degree tick marks, and Zero (0) and 45 degree tick marks.

Sky/Ground Fill The background of the attitude display is ﬁlled in sky and ground, divided by a horizon line. The horizon line and sky/ground ﬁll rotate in roll, and move vertically in pitch.

At or greater than 16 000 feet of altitude, and the pitch angle is +17 degrees or -7.5 degrees or greater, the sky/ground ﬁll stops displaying increased pitch. Instead, the sky/ground ﬁll with display a portion of both ground and sky not in relation to the actual horizon.

At less than 16 000 feet of altitude, and the pitch angle is +20.2 degrees or −7.5 degrees or greater, the sky/ground ﬁll stops displaying increased pitch.
