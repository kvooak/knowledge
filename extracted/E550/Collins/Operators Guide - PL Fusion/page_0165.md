Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics HSI Format

Preselect Vertical Deviation Scale

The preselect vertical deviation scale uses the active NAV source vertical deviation pointer. The scale comes into view automatically if the preselect NAV source becomes active and the scale was not in view. The deviation pointer is a cyan diamond.

Aircraft Symbol A white aircraft symbol is shown in the center of the Rose display with the nose pointed toward the lubber line. The aircraft symbol is a representation of the aircraft. The nose of the aircraft is placed at and represents the aircraft present position.

Bearing Pointer Two bearing pointers can be shown on the HSI display: one from number 1 bearing sources, and one from number 2 bearing sources. The bearing pointer from the number 1 source is shown as a cyan single-lined pointer. The bearing pointer from the number 2 source is shown as a white double-lined pointer. The bearing pointer sources are selected using the BRG1 and BRG2 selections in the BRG SRC dialog box. The selected bearing pointer sources are shown in the lower left corner of the HSI. The bearing source, bearing pointer icon, bearing distance, and bearing identiﬁer can all be shown in the bearing pointer data ﬁeld.
