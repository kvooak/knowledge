MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 DU – Chart Format Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• OUT OF DATE: shown in yellow if the chart is determined to be out of date as compared to aircraft system time.

• CHECK DATE: shown in yellow if aircraft system time is not available to determine chart currency.

• NO CHART SELECTED: shown in white if no chart was previously shown.

• Processing Request: shown in white if a chart request cannot be completed within 4 seconds of the operator’s request.

• Chart Fault: shown in white if the chart request is not completed after 35 seconds of the operator’s request.

No-Aircraft Symbol The no-aircraft symbol (a yellow aircraft with white circle and slash) shows if the FMS position, true heading, or magnetic variation data is missing. For non-WGS84 compliant charts, a green no-aircraft symbol shows.
