Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Visual Annunciations

PFD: Lateral Navigation Flag

#### .

A boxed red LNAV ﬂag is shown at the right end of the lateral deviation scale when LNAV data from the FMS or VOR is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.

The LNAV deviation pointer is removed when the LNAV ﬂag is being shown.

ADI: Radio Altitude Flag

#### .

A boxed red RAD ﬂag is shown to the left of the radio altitude digital readout when radio altitude data is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state. The RAD ﬂag is shown in the same location as the RAD comparator and has priority over the RAD comparator.

ADI: SVS Flag

#### .

If SVS is selected, a boxed red SVS ﬂag is shown to the right of the altitude scale when SVS data is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.

Deselecting SVS removes the SVS ﬂag.
