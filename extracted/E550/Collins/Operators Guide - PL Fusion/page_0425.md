Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 DISPLAY OPERATION Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Multifunction Keyboard Panel Operation

#### The FMS, ROUTE, MSG, DEP/ARR, D→(Direct-To), CHART, SYS, CNS, and CKLST buttons are Quick Access Keys (QAK). When pushed, a QAK commands a speciﬁc format, menu, or dialog box to show, and repositions the cursor to that format. The CAS button controls paging on the EICAS format.
