Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics HSI Format

Figure 5-26 HUD – Heading/Navigation Indicators

The HSI has 3 states of operation:

• CDI indications with overlays.

• FMS map indications with overlays.

• Clean HSI: All overlays removed.

The Horizontal Situation Indicator (HSI) format on the DU is a compass rose that shows heading, course, and track information. The HSI provides lubber line heading, a heading readout, selected heading bug, compass reference marks, course pointer, lateral deviation bar, scale, and to/from arrow. Pilot and copilot-side bearing pointers are available for display. A white aircraft symbol is shown in the center of the HSI.
