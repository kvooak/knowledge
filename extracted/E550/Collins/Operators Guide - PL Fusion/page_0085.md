Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 CONTROLS AND INDICATORS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Multifunction Keyboard Panel

### MULTIFUNCTION KEYBOARD PANEL

The Multifunction Keyboard Panel (MKP) includes an alphanumeric keypad for data entry, menu navigation buttons, COM and NAV buttons for radio control, and Quick Access Key (QAK) buttons for format control. Two MKP are installed on the center console, one for each side of the cockpit.
