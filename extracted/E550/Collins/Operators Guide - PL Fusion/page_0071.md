Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 CONTROLS AND INDICATORS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Audio Control Panel

ST Sidetone Volume Control

The ST knob controls the sidetone volume in the selected radio. Interphone sidetone volume is not affected by this control.

VOX Voice-Activated Interphone Control

The VOX knob provides on/off and sensitivity control for the voice-activated interphone. Pushing the knob in turns off the voice activation feature in the interphone and transfers control of interphone audio transmission to the INPH PTT button on the Cursor Control Panel (CCP) or control yoke. Unlatching the knob to the popped out position enables interphone hot mic and allow adjustment of the VOX sensitivity level. (Squelch Control) Rotating the knob clockwise decreases the VOX threshold sensitivity requiring louder inputs for VOX activation.

BOOM/AUTO/ MASK Oxygen Mask But- ton

In AUTO the oxygen mask microphone is automatically selected when the oxygen mask is donned and switches back to boom when the oxygen mask is stowed. The MASK or BOOM microphone can be manually selected by placing the knob in the MASK or BOOM position.

SPKR Flight Deck Speaker Con- trol

The SPKR knob controls the selection and volume for the ﬂight deck speakers. When pushed down (in), the on-side ﬂight deck speaker is muted except for aural voice and tone warnings. When unlatched to the up (out) position, the knob controls the volume of the audio broadcast over the ﬂight deck speakers. Volume of aural voice and tone warnings to the speakers cannot be adjusted by this knob.
