DISPLAY OPERATION Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 GUI Elements Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

• A boundary box is used to group GUI objects that are related, or to separate unrelated GUI objects.

• A caret icon indicates that the selection accesses a menu, submenu or dialog box, or another page within the same format or menu. The user can perform these operations by highlighting the DU caret icon with the cursor and selecting it.

• The Double Stack Knob (DSK) icon indicates when the DSK has unique functionality based on the present DU format. Cyan indicates an active knob, white indicates that the knob is active somewhere on the format, and gray indicates the knob is inactive on the format.
