Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Chart – Chart Task Menu

Chart Auto-Se- quence

Chart Auto-Sequence single stage toggle to enable or disable the chart auto-sequence feature. If enabled, the Chart format can automatically show the next chart in the following circumstances:

• The SID chart (deﬁned on the Terminal Charts: Origin menu) will automatically show upon takeoff (in-air and greater than 100 knots).

• The Airport chart (deﬁned on the Terminal Charts: Destination menu) will automatically show upon landing (on-ground and less than 80 knots).

Subscription Subscription is a submenu select to show the Chart Subscription dialog box.
