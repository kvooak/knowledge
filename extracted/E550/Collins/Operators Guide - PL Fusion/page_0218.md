MENUS AND DISPLAYS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Synoptic – Fuel Page Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

Figure 5-66 Synoptic – Fuel Page

The FUEL Synoptic page graphically represents the fuel system. The FUEL Synoptic page is accessed through the Synoptic drop down menu accessed with the SYS QAK. The FUEL Synoptic page displays the two wing tanks, connected to the engines and APU by pumps, ﬂow lines, and valves.

SELECTIONS:

Fuel Quantities Fuel quantities are depicted by digital readouts for the two wing tanks. A digital readout for the total fuel on board shows at the top of the page, along with a fuel used readout.

Fuel Temperatures Fuel temperatures are depicted by digital readouts for the right and left fuel lines for the engines.
