Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics AFCS Flight Mode Annunciations

The ﬂight path acceleration symbol is shown to the left of the FPV cue. The ﬂight path acceleration symbol moves above the wing of the ﬂight path symbol to indicate acceleration, and moves below the wing for deceleration.

Autopilot Field The AP/AT ﬁeld is split in three, top to bottom. The Autopilot (AP) engage status message shows on the top portion of the ﬁeld. A couple arrow shows in the middle of the ﬁeld. the Autothrottle (AT) engage status shows in the lower portion of the ﬁeld. Autopilot status messages and associated functions show in the list that follows:

• AP shows in green on both PFDs when the AP is engaged.

• AP shows in red upon disengagement of the autopilot for any reason.

• The AP ﬂashes to indicate a disengage.

N

NOTE

The AP disconnect alert is cancelled with the side-stick mounted AP Disconnect (DISC) switch. The autopilot can also be reengaged in order to cancel the disengage alert.

The couple arrow shows in the middle of the AP engage ﬁeld. The couple arrow shows in the same color as the AP engage/disengage indication, when present, and ﬂashes when the AP engage/disengage indication ﬂashes. When the AP engaged/disengaged indication is not in view, the couple arrow shows in white and does not ﬂash.
