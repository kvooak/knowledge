Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics Synoptic – Flight Control Page

Figure 5-64 Synoptic – Flight Control Ground Page

The FLIGHT CONTROLS Synoptic page graphically represents the ﬂight controls. The FLIGHT CONTROLS Synoptic page is accessed through the Synoptic drop down menu accessed with the SYS QAK. The synoptic page provides a list of control surfaces at the bottom of the page. The list includes rudder, elevator, aileron, and spoiler. On the ground horizontal stabilizer and ﬂap indications are added. Square boxes indicate each control surface. Filled square means it is in operation currently. Unﬁlled squares depict a control surface not in operation currently. Green indicators depict a properly working control surface, while yellow indicators depict a malfunctioning control surface.

SELECTIONS:

Ailerons The ailerons are depicted by icons on the wings. The icons are green when functioning properly, and turn into yellow X when not functioning properly. The ailerons are also indicated on either side of an AILERON legend.
