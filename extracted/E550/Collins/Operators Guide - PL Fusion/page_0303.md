### MAP – VERTICAL SITUATION DISPLAY

Figure 5-116 Map – VSD (Aircraft Centered)

The Vertical Situation Display (VSD) provides a side view of the vertical proﬁle ﬂown by the aircraft. Typically, the vertical proﬁle is referenced to the ﬂight plan, but can also be referenced to the current aircraft track. The vertical proﬁle provides supplemental awareness to the lateral maps for VNAV operation and Terrain surveillance.

The VSD can show the following elements:

• Terrain

• Flight plan types:

• Modiﬁed

• Active

• Missed approach preview.

• Flight plan data:

• Waypoints

• Altitude constraints

• Flight plan legs

• Conditional waypoints

• Discontinuity

• Runway sideview

• Final approach ﬁx

• Approach feather.
