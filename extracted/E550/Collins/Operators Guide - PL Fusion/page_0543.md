#### MANUAL DATA ENTRY RANGES AND SELECTIONS

The following shows the range or selection list choices for manual entry of data into each CPDLC data ﬁeld. The following callouts apply:

ALT (Level):

• Altitude expressed in either feet or ﬂight level

• Single or Block Altitude

• Displays as feet from 600 – 70000, resolution of 10

• Displays as FL from 30 – 700, resolution of 1.

ETA (Time):

• Estimated time of arrival

• Expressed as HHMM.

TIME:

• Time value

• Expressed as HHMM.

FIX (Position):

• Navigation ﬁx expressed in alphanumeric characters

• Displays from 1 – 5 characters.

AIRPORT:

• 3 or 4 letter ICAO Airport

• ID expressed in capital letters.

LAT/LON (Position):

• Latitude and longitude in whole degrees, degrees, minutes and seconds, or degrees and minutes

• Written as {N or S} 0–90 {E or W} 0–180 or {N or S} 0–89 00–59.99 {E or W} 0–180 00–59.99 or {N or S} 0–89 [0–59] {E or W} 0–180 [0–59]. (For example, N40E110 or N4004.76E11040.55).
