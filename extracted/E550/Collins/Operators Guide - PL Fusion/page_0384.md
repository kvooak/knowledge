MESSAGES AND ANNUNCIATORS Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 Visual Annunciations Pro Line Fusion® Avionics

ADI: Airspeed Flag

#### .

A boxed red IAS warning ﬂag is shown in place of the airspeed pointer and digital readout when airspeed data is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.

The airspeed digital readout, airspeed scale, trend vector, overspeed marker, low speed markers, airspeed bug, and VSPEED bugs are all removed when the IAS ﬂag is being shown.

ADI: Altitude Flag

#### .

A boxed red ALT ﬂag is shown in place of the barometric altitude pointer and digital readout when altitude data is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.

The barometric altitude scale, digital readout, and metric altitude indication are all removed when the ALT ﬂag is being shown.

ADI: Attitude Flag

#### .

A boxed red ATT ﬂag is shown in place of the attitude indicator when attitude data is missing or invalid. The ﬂag ﬂashes for 5 seconds and then changes to a steady-on state.

The attitude indicator, pitch tape, roll scale, and roll pointer are all removed when the ATT ﬂag is being shown.
