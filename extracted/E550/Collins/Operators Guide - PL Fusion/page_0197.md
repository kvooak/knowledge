Embraer Legacy 450/500 and Praetor 500/600 MENUS AND DISPLAYS Pro Line Fusion® Avionics EICAS Format

• Analog scale – The ITT analog scale shows as a white arc. A green pointer displays the current ITT value. A yellow tick shows at the ITT caution value, and a red tick shows at the ITT redline value.

• Digital readout – The ITT digital readout displays the current ITT value in the center of the analog scale.

• Ignition status – A green IGN shows on the outside of either gauge when ignition occurs, or cyan IGN OFF when the igniters are turned off.

• An inverse red FIRE indication shows when the system detects an engine ﬁre.

Secondary Engine Indications

The EICAS also tracks secondary engine indications including Engine High Pressure Compressor/Turbine (N2), oil pressure, oil temperature, and vibration.

• The N2 display consists of digital readouts for each engine and a white N2 legend.
